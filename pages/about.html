About me

I am a research faculty at TrEE in the TIMC laboratory in Grenoble. I am interested in machine learning and causal inference methods to better understand gene regulatory networks, with a particular focus on how the 3D structure of the genome affects and is affected by gene regulation.

Scientific computing activities

I am a contributor in a number of scientific computing libraries in Python including:

  • scikit-learn - Machine learning in Python.
  • matplotlib - a python 2D plotting library.

I am also involved in the French speaking Python association, AFPy, and Software Carpentry.
